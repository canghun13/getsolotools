Payment Received Confirmation Email Template

A payment confirmation email is a small but professional touch that closes the loop, gives the client a record, and leaves the relationship on a positive note. Keep it brief and warm.

Email Template — click to copy
Subject:Payment Received - Invoice #[001]
Hi [Client Name], Just a quick note to confirm I've received your payment of $[amount] for Invoice #[001]. Thank you! The invoice is now fully settled. A receipt is attached for your records. It was a pleasure working with you on [project name]. I hope we get the chance to work together again. Best, [Your Name]

Tips for This Email

  • Send this the same day you receive payment — timeliness signals professionalism.
  • Attach a receipt or the marked-paid invoice for the client's accounting records.
  • Use this as a natural moment to express interest in future work.
  • Keep it short — two to three sentences is enough.
