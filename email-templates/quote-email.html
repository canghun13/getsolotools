Freelance Quote Email Template

A quote email should be short and clear. The client asked for a number — give it to them quickly, with just enough context to understand what's included. Save the full detail for the contract or proposal stage.

Email Template — click to copy
Subject:Quote for [Project Name] - [Your Name/Business]
Hi [Client Name], Thanks for reaching out. Based on what you've shared about [project description], here's my quote: [Service/Deliverable]: $[amount] [Service/Deliverable]: $[amount] Total estimate: $[total] This covers [brief scope description]. The quote is valid for [14 days]. Timeline: approximately [X weeks] from project start. Let me know if you have questions or if you'd like to adjust the scope. Happy to hop on a quick call to discuss. Best, [Your Name]

Tips for This Email

  • Break down the quote by deliverable so the client can see where the cost comes from.
  • Set a validity period — quotes that never expire let clients stall indefinitely.
  • Include the timeline alongside the price so they understand what they're buying.
  • Offer to adjust scope — it opens a negotiation that keeps the conversation moving.
