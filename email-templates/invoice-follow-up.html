Invoice Follow-Up Email Template (Friendly Reminder)

Most late payments aren't intentional — invoices get buried, forwarded to accounting, or simply forgotten. A friendly follow-up email is usually all it takes. Send this 1-3 days after the due date passes.

Email Template
Subject: Friendly Reminder: Invoice #[001] Due [Date]
Hi [Client Name], Just a quick follow-up on Invoice #[001] for [project name], totaling $[amount], which was due on [due date]. If you've already sent payment, please ignore this — and thank you! If not, I'd appreciate it if you could process it at your earliest convenience. [Optional: re-include payment link or details] Let me know if you have any questions. Best, [Your Name]

Tips for This Email

  • Send this 1-3 business days after the due date. Don't wait weeks before following up.
  • Keep the tone light and assume good faith — this is still a relationship worth protecting.
  • Include the invoice number and amount so they can locate it immediately.
  • Re-attach or re-link the invoice in case the original was lost.
  • If you have a payment link, include it again — remove any friction to paying.
