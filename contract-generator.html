Additional Notes

Why use a contract?

A contract protects both parties by setting clear expectations upfront — scope, payment, deadlines, and what happens if things go wrong.

Is this legally binding?

A signed contract is legally binding in most jurisdictions. For complex projects, consult a lawyer. For standard freelance work, this covers the essentials.

How to get it signed

Download the PDF and send it via DocuSign, HelloSign, or PandaDoc for e-signatures. Always get it signed before starting work.

What's a kill fee?

A kill fee is charged when a client cancels mid-project. It compensates you for time spent and opportunity cost. 25–50% of remaining value is standard.
