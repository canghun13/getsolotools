Our Story

Built for the solo worker, by someone who gets it.

GetSoloTools is a free collection of practical tools for freelancers, consultants, and independent professionals who need to get things done — without subscriptions or bloat.

Why GetSoloTools exists

Running solo means wearing every hat — designer, accountant, marketer, and project manager — all at once. Most tools are built for teams, which means they're over-engineered, expensive, or require a login just to generate an invoice.


GetSoloTools is different. Every tool here is free, runs in your browser, and does exactly what it says on the tin. No accounts. No upsells. No data collection. Just tools that actually help.

What you'll find here

  • An Invoice Generator that lets you create, preview, and download professional PDFs in under two minutes.
  • A Receipt Generator for quick proof-of-payment documents your clients actually trust.
  • A Quote Generator to send polished project estimates before the work even begins.
  • An Hourly Rate Calculator to figure out what you should actually be charging.
  • More tools added regularly — all free, all instant.
