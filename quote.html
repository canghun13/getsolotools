What is a quote?

A quote (or estimate) is a document you send to a potential client before work begins, outlining the scope and cost of a project. It sets clear expectations for both sides.

Quote vs Invoice

A quote comes before the work, proposing a price. An invoice comes after, requesting payment. A client approves a quote, then you deliver the work and send an invoice.

Tips for winning quotes

Be specific about deliverables. Include a validity date to create urgency. Break down line items clearly so clients see the value they're getting.
