What makes a great proposal?

The best proposals are specific, not generic. Name the client's problem clearly, show exactly what you'll deliver, and make the pricing feel like a no-brainer relative to the value.

Always include a deposit

A 30–50% upfront deposit protects you and filters out low-commitment clients. It also gives you runway to actually start the work without chasing payment mid-project.

Limit revision rounds

Scope creep kills freelance profitability. Specify exactly how many revision rounds are included — typically 2 — and what counts as an additional revision vs. a new request.
