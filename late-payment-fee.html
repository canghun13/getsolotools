Put it in your contract

Late fees are only enforceable if stated in your contract or invoice terms. Add a clause like "1.5% monthly interest on overdue balances" before you start work.

Common late fee rates

Most freelancers charge 1.5% per month (18% annually). Some use a flat fee of $25–$50/month. Both are standard and legally defensible in most jurisdictions.

When to escalate

If an invoice is 60+ days overdue, consider sending a formal demand letter, using a collections service, or filing in small claims court for amounts under $10,000.
