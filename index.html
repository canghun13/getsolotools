How to create an invoice

Fill in your business details, add your client's information, list your services or products, and click Download PDF. Your invoice is ready in under 2 minutes.

What should an invoice include?

A proper invoice needs: your business name and contact, client details, a unique invoice number, issue and due dates, itemized services, and the total amount due.

Is this tool really free?

Yes, completely free with no sign-up required. You can create unlimited invoices and download them as PDFs at no cost, forever.

Invoice vs Receipt

An invoice is sent before payment to request money owed. A receipt is issued after payment as proof of purchase. Both serve different purposes in your business records.
