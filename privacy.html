1. Overview

GetSoloTools ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website at getsolotools.com. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding your data.

📋 The short version: Our tools run entirely in your browser. We don't see, receive, or store any data you enter into our tools — ever.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

When you use any tool on GetSoloTools (Invoice Generator, Receipt Generator, etc.), all processing happens locally in your web browser. We do not collect:

  • Names, business names, or addresses you enter into tools
  • Invoice amounts, line items, or financial information
  • Any generated PDFs or documents
  • Email addresses (unless you contact us directly)
  • Account information (we have no accounts)

3. Information We May Collect

We collect only basic, anonymous usage data to understand how our site performs:

  • Analytics data — page views, general location (country/region), browser type, and device type via Google Analytics. This data is aggregated and anonymous.
  • Log data — standard server logs (IP address, browser type, referring page) that are retained briefly and not linked to any individual.
  • Cookies — Google Analytics may set cookies to distinguish unique visits. You can disable these via your browser settings or a cookie opt-out tool.

4. Google AdSense

We may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site or other sites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

5. Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to third-party services (such as FreshBooks or QuickBooks). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies before use.

6. Children's Privacy

GetSoloTools is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy
